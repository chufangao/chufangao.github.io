Hello! I am 3rd year PhD Candidate at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Professor Jimeng Sun. My focus areas include Information Extraction, Tabular Data, and Machine Learning for Healthcare. I am also broadly interested in less-than-supervised Machine Learning and memory-augmented models.

In my Masters, I was advised by Professor Artur Dubrawski at Carnegie Mellon University as a part of AutonLab where I completed my thesis: Addressing Time-series Signal Quality in Healthcare Data. I enjoy working on projects that involve a combination of machine learning, natural language processing, and healthcare data. Shoot me a message on Linkedin or email if you want to chat!
