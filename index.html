Hello! I am 2nd year PhD Student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Professor Jimeng Sun. My areas of focus include Natural Language Processing, Weak Supervision, Sequential Event Data, and Machine Learning for Healthcare in general. I am also broadly interested in time-series data and less than supervised Machine Learning.

In my Masters, I was advised by Professor Artur Dubrawski at Carnegie Mellon University as a part of AutonLab.
